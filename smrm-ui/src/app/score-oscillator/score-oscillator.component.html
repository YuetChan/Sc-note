Stock Market Risk Meter Oscillator

Source: yuetcheukchan@gmail.com | Author: Yuet Chan

The oscillator reflects the average score of selected stocks from manufacturing, consumer services, consumer staples, transportation, and commercial real estate, serving as a proxy for economic health. A score above 6 signals low risk, suggesting strong market conditions and a favorable time to invest. A score below 2 indicates high risk, warning of unfavorable conditions for buying stocks.
